About Us

Brandiac Ltd. serves as a holding for e-commerce stores. The start-up owns and runs two e-commerce stores and will create more in the future.

Branding

A lot of work and effort is put into the e-commerce stores that Brandiac owns. The logo has to fit the brand identity of the store, all the brands of the stores need to radiate what they offer and all the customers need to feel what the brands are all about.

Creating Products

Brandiac puts a lot of time and effort in finding the right product manufacturers. The products need to be high quality and environmentally friendly made. Also, the price range of the products are not allowed to be too expensive for the customers.

Marketing

The marketing is made with Facebooks Ads and Google Analytics. The ads of the stores can be seen on Facebook, Instagram, Youtube and Google, and that way Brandiac makes sure that the stores get not only a lot of traffic, but also many sales.

Shipping

Brandiac works together with several logistics companies, that make sure that the products are stored and delivered. The delivery usually takes about 2-5 working days and is environmentally friendly.

Shops
